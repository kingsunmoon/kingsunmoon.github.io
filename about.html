Nhat Nguyet Hoang

I am a Melbourne based creative practicing in the fields of animation, illustration and digital design. My works encompass a broad range of abstract, experimental, advertising and character animation styles. I have a professional background in the advertising industry, having worked across all facets of production with multiple high-profile studios: Passion Pictures Melbourne, TBWA, Ogilvy, and the STUDIO New York and big clients: Samsung, Abbott, HealthIQ, Vinamilk.

My personal animation works have been screened in cities around the world - namely in New York, Mumbai, Malmo and Madrid, as well as here in Melbourne at the 2019 MIAF (Melbourne International Animation Festival).
